Privacy



Upon registering/ signing up, Users are required to provide their email address which then gets encrypted and used as their User ID. Beside registration date and last login date, Relieve only keep the hash (encrypted version) of Users email address, not the actual email address.

Relieve also may collect Users biodata (GENDER, AGE, PROFESSION) without user real name when user submit to Relieve. Relieve only use the collected information to make psychologists easier to identify the problem from the users biodata and background
